Ella`s Hearty Lasagna Recipe

    Materials Needed

    • 9x13 inch baking dish (deep-dish preferred)
    • Large pot (for boiling noodles)
    • Large skillet or Dutch oven (for sauce)
    • Mixing bowl
    • Aluminum foil
    • Wooden spoon or spatula
    • Grater (for cheese)

    Ingredients

    1. Meat Sauce
    2. 1 lb Ground Beef (lean)
    3. 1/2 lb Ground Italian Sausage (Sweet or hot)
    4. 1 medium Yellow Onion, diced
    5. 3 cloves garlic, minced
    6. 1 (28-oz) can crushed tomatoes or marinara sauce
    7. 1 (6-oz) can tomato paste
    8. 1 tsp Italian seasoning or dried basil
    9. Salt and pepper
    10. 1 tbsp Olive oil
    11. 1 tsp sugar
    12. lasagna noodles
    13. Ricotta Cheese or Cottage Cheese
    14. 1 large egg, slightly beaten
    15. shredded mozzarella cheese
    16. grated parmesan cheese
    17. fresh parsley

    Procedures:

    1. Prepare the Meat Sauce

      1. Brown Meat: Heat olive oil in a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat. Add ground beef and Italian sausage. Cook until light brown, breaking it apart with a spoon.
      2. Add Aromatics: Add diced onions and cook until softened (about 3-4 minutes). Add minced garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.
      3. Simmer: Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning. Add sugar (if using), salt, and pepper to taste. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for at least 20–30 minutes (up to 1 hour for deeper flavor), stirring occasionally.

    2. Prepare Noodles and Cheese

      1. Noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook noodles according to package directions until al dente (firm to the bite). Drain and lay them flat on parchment paper or foil to prevent sticking.
      2. Cheese Mixture: In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten egg, 1/2 cup of Parmesan cheese, and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese. Stir in parsley if using.

    3. Assemble the Lasagna (Preheat oven to 375°F/190°C)

      1. Base Layer: Spread about 1 cup of the meat sauce on the bottom of the 9x13 pan to prevent sticking.
      2. Layer 1: Place a layer of noodles over the sauce (usually 3-4 noodles, slightly overlapping). Spread 1/3 of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles. Top with 1/3 of the remaining mozzarella and 1.5 cups of meat sauce.
      3. Layers 2 & 3: Repeat the layering process (noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, mozzarella) two more times.
      4. Final Top Layer: Top with the last of the noodles, the remaining meat sauce, and the last of the mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

    4. Bake

      1. Cover: Spray a sheet of aluminum foil with cooking spray (to prevent cheese from sticking) and cover the dish tightly.
      2. Bake: Bake in the preheated oven for 25–30 minutes.
      3. Brown: Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10–15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbling and golden brown.
      4. Rest: Crucial Step: Let the lasagna rest at room temperature for at least 15–20 minutes before cutting. This allows the layers to set so it doesn't fall apart when served.
